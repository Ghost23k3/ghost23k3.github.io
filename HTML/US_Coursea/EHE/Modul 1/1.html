Understanding the Need for Security

Video

Information is different than data. It has meaning. We want to secure it. We know the possible theft, disruption, ..., so we want to have a minimal risk or where it is tolerable.


One reason is the fast development of the device, due to modern technology. So, in some cases we leave security behind. New technology is not focused on security, it is more focused on easy use.


We also rely on computers for everything. (Storing Information, Network Environments, ...)


What is Information Security?

Information Security is a state of well-being of information and infrastructure in which the possibility of theft, tampering, and disruption of information and services is low or tolerable.
